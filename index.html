🔢 Factor Catcher

A fast-paced math catching game!
Sharpen your skills with factors, multiples, primes & more.

How to Play

  • 🎯 Each round shows a rule (e.g. "Catch multiples of 3").
  • 🔢 Numbered balls fall from the top.
  • 🧺 Move the basket left/right with ← → keys, A/D, or by dragging on touch screens.
  • ✅ Catch numbers that match the rule to earn points.
  • ❌ Catching a wrong number costs you a life (❤️).
  • ⏱️ Each round has a time limit — survive 10 rounds!
  • ⭐ Earn up to 3 stars based on your final score.
